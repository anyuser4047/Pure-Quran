    Your Learning Journey

    Assessment

    Begin with a free evaluation session where our teacher assesses your current level and recommends the appropriate starting point.

    Foundation

    Build strong fundamentals with proper pronunciation (Makharij), basic tajweed rules, and fluency in reading.

    Application

    Apply tajweed rules in Quranic recitation, memorize selected surahs, and understand their meanings.

    Mastery

    Progress to advanced recitation, complete memorization (Hifz), or obtain Ijazah certification in Quranic recitation.
